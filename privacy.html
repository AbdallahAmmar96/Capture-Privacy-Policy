Privacy Policy

Capture — Free Screen Recorder & Screenshot

Your captures stay on your device. No accounts, no uploads, no tracking.

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Capture Chrome extension ("Capture", "the extension", "we") handles your information. Capture is a local-first tool: it records your screen and takes screenshot entirely within your browser. We do not operate servers that receive your content, and we do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

✔ No data leaves your device iv>

1. The short version

  • Every screenshot and recording is processed and stored locally on your computer.
  • We do not upload your captures, browsing history, or any personal data to us or any third party.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.

2. What the extension stores (on your device only)

To work, Capture saves the following on your own machine, using your browser's local storage and download folder. None of it is transmitted to us.

DataWhere it's storedWhy
Your screenshots & recordings Browser local storage (IndexedDB) and your Downloads folder So you can view, edit, re-open, and re-download your captures
Capture library index (titles, tags, source page title/URL, file size, date) chrome.storage (local) To list, search, and organize your captures To remember your preferences between sessions

You can delete any capture from the in-extension Library at any time, or remove all stored data by uninstalling the extension.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.).
  • We do not collect your browsing history or the content of pages you visit.
  • We do not collect health, financial, location, or authentication information.
  • We do not collect user activity, analytics, or usage metrics.
  • We do not transmit your screenshots, recordings, microphone, or camera data to any server.

4. Camera, microphone, and screen access

When you choose to record, the extension may access your screen, a browser tab, a microphone, or a camera — but only after you explicitly start a recording and grant the relevant browser permission. These media streams are processed locally to produce your recording and are never sent to us or any third party. Recording stops when you stop it.

5. Why the extension requests its permissions

Capture requests only the permissions needed for its screen-capture and screenshot features. Each is used solely to provide functionality on your device:

6. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone, because we never receive it in the first place. The extension makes no third-party network calls and contains no advertising or tracking SDKs.

7. Children's privacy

Capture is a general-purpose utility and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised version at the same URL. Continued use of the extension after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

This is the core screen-recording feature.
