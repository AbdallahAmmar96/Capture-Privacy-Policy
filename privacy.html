This Privacy Policy explains how the Capture Chrome extension ("Capture", "the extension", "we") handles your information. Capture is a local-first tool: it records your screen and takes screenshots entirely within your browser. We do not operate servers that receive your content, and we do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

✔ No data leaves your device ✔ No account required ✔ No analytics or tracking ✔ No ads

1. The short version

  • Every screenshot and recording is processed and stored locally on
  • We do not upload your captures, browsing history, or any personal data to us or any third party.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.

2. What the extension stores (on your device only)

To work, Capture saves the following on your own machine, using your browser's local storage and download folder. None of it is transmitted to us.

DataWhere it's storedWhy
Your screenshots & recordings Browser local storage (IndexedDB) and your Downloads folder So you can view, edit, re-open, and re-download your captures
Capture library index (titles, tags, source page title/URL, file size, date) chrome.storage (local) To list, search, and organize your captures
Your settings (quality, format, fps, filename template, theme, selected camera/mic, notifications) chrome.storage (local) To remember your preferences between sessions

You can delete any capture from the in-extension Library at any time, or remove all stored data by uninstalling the extension.

4. Camera, microphone, and screen access

When you choose to record, the extension may access your screen, a browser tab, a microphone, or a camera — but only after you explicitly start a recording and grant the relevant browser permission. These media streams are processed locally to produce your recording and are never sent to us or any third party. Recording stops when you stop it.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone, because we never receive it in the first place. The extension makes no third-party network calls and contains no advertising or tracking SDKs.
